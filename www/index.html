Quick Tips for Police Encounters

  1. Hands out of pockets
  2. Be as respecful as possible
  3. Keep hands out of pockets
  4. Be as cooperative as possible
  5. Be completely honest
  6. Ask to see the police officers badge

Quick Tips for Suspicious Group Encounters

  1. Don't turn your back
  2. Stay in public areas
  3. Always be perceptive of surroundings
  4. Know you're town
  5. Don't run
  6. Don't claim an area (group or gang)
